
Standards for creating, formatting, and using visual content effectively.

## Text in Images

Avoid placing critical information in images. Text embedded in images is not searchable, indexable, or accessible to screen readers. Users cannot copy, translate, or search for this content.

## Alternatives to Images

### Mermaid.js

[Mermaid.js](https://mermaid.js.org/) is a JavaScript-based diagramming tool that renders text-based definitions into diagrams. Popular for its simplicity and version control compatibility, it supports flowcharts, sequence diagrams, Gantt charts, and more.

**Common patterns:**

- Flowcharts and decision trees
- Sequence diagrams
- Architecture diagrams
- State diagrams
- Entity relationship diagrams
